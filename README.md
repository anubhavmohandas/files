## üìù What to Include

### Work Experience
- Full job title and company name
- Dates (use "Present" for current roles)
- 3-5 bullet points per job with quantifiable achievements
- Technologies or tools used

### Skills
- 10-15 technical skills relevant to your field
- 5-8 soft skills (leadership, communication, etc.)
- Certifications and their dates
- Languages with proficiency levels

### Projects (Optional but Recommended)
- Personal or professional projects
- Technologies used
- Links to GitHub, live demos, or portfolios
- Brief description of impact
